shubman gill

Shubman Gill is a right-handed Indian cricketer known for his elegant batting and is considered one of the sport's brightest young talents. He achieved significant fame after being named the Player of the Tournament at the 2018 U19 World Cup, which India won. Gill holds several records, including being the youngest to score a double century in ODIs and the fastest to reach 2000 and 2500 runs in ODIs. He also plays for the Gujarat Titans in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has won the IPL and ICC Champions Trophy.
